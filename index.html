How to Use Beacon

Follow these simple steps to get started with our app!

1

Create Your Account

Download Beacon and sign up with your email and password. Set up your profile by adding basic information to personalize your experience.

2

Add Your Deadlines

After your profile is all set up, move on over to our calendar page to start adding deadlines and events. Enter course names, assignment due dates, exam schedules, and any additional important events.

3

Check Your Dashboard

The home screen welcomes you and shows your upcoming deadlines at a glance. Navigate through your schedule to see what's going on today, what is coming up, and what has passed.

4

Navigate Your Calendar

Use the calendar view to see all your deadlines and events in a monthly format. Tap any date to see the listed information about assignments or events scheduled for that day.

5

Manage Your Events

View event details, edit deadlines, or delete events as needed. Keep your calendar up-to-date by managing your assignments and changing dates as needed.

6

Connect with Friends

Search for friends, send connection requests, and manage your friend list. Share assignments and events with friends for easier access to group activities.

7

Import from LMS

Save time by importing your calendar directly from Brightspace, Canvas, or other Learning Management Systems and all your course deadlines sync automatically!
